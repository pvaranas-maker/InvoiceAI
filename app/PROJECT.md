# InvoiceAI

## Mission

Build the easiest AI office manager for small businesses.

## Target Users

- Plumbers
- Electricians
- Landscapers
- HVAC
- Contractors
- Freelancers

## Goal

Allow business owners to simply describe the work they completed.

Example:

> Installed water heater for John Smith. Labor $350. Parts $420.

The AI should automatically:

- Create invoice
- Save customer
- Email invoice
- Add payment link
- Track payment
- Update bookkeeping
